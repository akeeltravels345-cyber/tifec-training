Caring for the Elderly

Led by Georgia Isaacs — Allied Health Professional with 8+ years of experience and 3+ years teaching at university level.

Caring for an older person well takes more than kindness — it takes skill. This course gives you both: the practical procedures and the human understanding to support elderly clients with dignity, confidence, and genuine care. CPR training is included.

What you'll learn

  • The role and responsibilities of a caregiver
  • Communicating effectively with elderly clients
  • The physical and psychological changes of aging
  • Elder abuse awareness
  • Monitoring vital signs
  • Essential care procedures
  • CPR
