HR & People

Introduction to HR Management

Led by Dr. Donnet O'Connor, who holds a PhD in psychology and brings nearly 20 years of HR experience in the Cayman Islands.

Human resources is where strategy meets people, the skill set every organisation needs and every career benefits from. Whether you manage a team, already handle HR tasks, or are stepping into a dedicated HR role, this course blends theory with real-world application so you leave able to recruit fairly, manage performance honestly, and become the person an organisation trusts with its most important asset: its people.

Who it's for

Team leaders and managers, anyone who already handles HR tasks alongside another role, and people moving into a dedicated HR position for the first time. No prior HR qualification is needed.

What you'll learn

By the end of this course you'll be able to:

  • Understand the strategic role of HR in any organisation
  • Navigate equal opportunity laws and compliance
  • Conduct job analysis and write effective job descriptions
  • Recruit, interview, and select the right candidates
  • Manage performance and conduct appraisals
  • Design training and development programmes
  • Understand compensation, benefits, and incentives
  • Build and sustain employee motivation and commitment

Course content

The programme runs across twelve modules, moving from foundations to advanced practice.

Core modules

  • Strategic Role of HRM
  • Equal Opportunity & the Law
  • The HR Scorecard
  • Job Analysis & Planning
  • Personnel Planning & Recruitment
  • Employee Testing & Selection

Advanced modules

  • Interviewing Candidates
  • Performance Management & Appraisal
  • Training & Developing Employees
  • Managing Your Career
  • Pay for Performance & Incentives
  • Benefits & Services

Your facilitator

Dr. Donnet O'Connor holds a PhD in psychology and brings nearly twenty years of HR experience across a range of industries in the Cayman Islands. Her background gives her a deep understanding of what really drives people at work, and she draws on real workplace scenarios to bring HR concepts to life, so you leave with both the knowledge and the practical confidence to use it. You won't just learn the theory. You'll learn how it plays out in local workplaces, from the interview room to the boardroom.
