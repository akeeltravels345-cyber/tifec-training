Introduction to HR Management

Led by Donnet O'Connor — nearly 20 years of HR experience in the Cayman Islands.

Human resources is where strategy meets people. This course gives you a complete, working foundation — the knowledge to recruit fairly, manage performance honestly, and become the person an organisation trusts with its most important asset.
