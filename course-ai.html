AI for Business & Career

Facilitator TBC · 8 weeks, hands-on and practical.

AI is changing every workplace in Cayman. This hands-on, practical course gives you a real edge: the tools, the judgement, and the confidence to use AI to grow a business, advance your career, or land a better job. No coding and no technical background needed, just practical skills you can use from day one.

Who it's for

Working professionals, business owners, and school leavers, learning side by side. Whatever your goal, the course is built around it:

  • Grow or launch a business using AI
  • Advance your career and become more valuable using AI
  • Position yourself better for a raise, a promotion, or a new job using AI

What you'll learn

By the end of this course you'll be able to:

  • Explain how AI really works, in plain English with no jargon
  • Use at least five AI tools confidently across work, business, and creative tasks
  • Critically evaluate AI outputs for accuracy and bias before you act on them
  • Design an AI-powered workflow or strategy for your sector, career, or business
  • Apply AI to grow a business, advance your career, or strengthen a job application

Course content

Eight weeks, built around the sectors that drive Cayman's economy:

  • Week 1: AI foundations, what it is and why now
  • Week 2: Prompting and personal productivity
  • Week 3: AI in healthcare and social care
  • Week 4: AI in financial services, legal, and compliance
  • Week 5: AI in tourism, hospitality, and retail
  • Week 6: AI in marketing, content, and brand
  • Week 7: Launch or uplevel a business with AI
  • Week 8: Ethics, responsible use, and your capstone project

This curriculum and its start date are being finalised, so some details may change before the course begins.
